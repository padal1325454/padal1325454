## 👋 Professional Summary

Software Engineer with **production experience** building React-powered analytics platforms in the PropTech industry. At **CRANTYA**, I develop **ListTrac** — a real estate listing analytics platform delivering unbiased traffic reporting and marketing intelligence to MLS brokers and agents nationwide. My work spans shipping new features, integrating real estate data APIs, optimizing frontend performance, and keeping production reliable at scale.

Beyond my day job, I build full-stack systems, 3D browser applications, and run ML analysis on real-world datasets. Currently completing an **MS in Computer and Information Sciences** at the University of North Texas, I'm looking to join a team where strong engineering and real-world impact go hand in hand.
